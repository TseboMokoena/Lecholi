About Us

Who are we?

Lecholi (Pty) Ltd is a Lesotho company established in June 2020 as a meat market for Basotho livestock farmers and a solution to meat customers. Currently Lecholi provides butchery services, braai solutions, events catering and management. We also provide these services as mobile, under our motto, "deliciously at your Doorstep." Here we deliver products and braai services where our client needs them. Our company also has events organisation leg, where we do events on our own or collaborate with other event organisers. We strive to be the most reliable and creative event organisers in Lesotho. We make sure our clients get the best of services at the most cost effective rate.

Our Vision

To build delicious and unique customer experience.

Our Mission

To serve customers with reliable timely, responsive and creative quality services.

Our Values

Customer satisfaction – we strive to meet clients’ anticipations by delivering on our promises Integrity – honesty, truth, and accuracy in our actions is our priority. Reliability - we are able to perform and maintain our functions in routine circumstances, as well as hostile or unexpected circumstances.

Our Commitment

We commit to stick to our vision, mission and values as we work on each occasion from start to finish at all times regardless of the size of the service.

Services

Meat products

We supply a wide range of quality meat products including beef, sausages, pork, chicken, chevon and mutton to customers as raw or flame grilled. We also supply in bulk to restaurants and other butcheries.

Events Braai Catering

We provide catering services to groups, parties and any social gatherings where we do braai at the place convenient to our clients. We have the capacity and ability serve large corporates and events.

Events Management

We provide events services to companies/individuals and also collaborate with other event organisers. Our most popular events include The Mountain Drive and Braai Experience and Inter-Company X-Mas party.
