Mountain drive & braai experience

Lekhalong la Baroa, Lesotho 28-29 September 2024

Welcome to Lecholi Mountain Drive & Braai Experience!

An annual outdoor initiative hosted by Lecholi (Pty) Ltd, offering a thrilling adventure that combines a vehicle convoy from Maseru town, a 4x4 mountain drive, an overnight camp, and of course, a delicious braai accompanied by music performances. This event has gained immense popularity and continues to grow, offering even more exciting activities for locals and international guests.

Join us at Lekhalong la Baroa, affectionately known as Khalong la Baroa (Bushmen’s Pass). This breathtaking spot provides the perfect backdrop for our event. Surrounded by nature, with expansive views and adventurous terrain, Khalong la Baroa is an ideal setting for unforgettable experiences. Whether you’re a thrill-seeker or a nature lover, there’s something for everyone!
